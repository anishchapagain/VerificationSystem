The Mathematical Engine: Siamese Networks

Unlike standard image classifiers that identify "what" an object is, a Siamese Network determines "distance" between two objects. It maps signatures into a high-dimensional latent space where genuine signatures of the same person are clustered tightly together, and forgeries are pushed far apart.
